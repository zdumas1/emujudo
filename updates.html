EMU Judo Shines at Sambo Tournament

The Judo Team recently participated in the 2025 Great Lakes Sambo Championship! We had two athletes get gold medals and two athletes get silver. All four athletes had no prior experience in Sambo and all placed. We are proud of our team!

New Semester Approaches!

School is returning at Eastern Michigan University on August 25, 2025. This means EMU Judo will also officially be back in action! Tuesday and Thursday practices will resume at 6 PM in Warner Gymnasium - Gym C. We look forward to seeing both new and returning members!
